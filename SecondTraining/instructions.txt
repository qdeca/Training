The goal is to make the datamodel of a clinic to be able to schedule appointments

- There are three types of persons to represent, the Patient, the Nurse and the Doctor
- The Appointment class concerns at least a Patient and a Doctor, during a set date, and can also concern several nurses in case of an intervention.

The objectives are as follow :

- Create the datamodel as described above
- Create a "main" method and instantiate the objects needed to create an appointment
- Display on the console the patient, the doctor and the date of the appointment
